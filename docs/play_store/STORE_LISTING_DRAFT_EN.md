Fun Diary

A safe and playful journaling app that helps children build a daily writing habit with Gunbi.

## Long description draft

Fun Diary is a warm and simple journaling app designed to help children build a writing habit and express their feelings safely. With Gunbi, children can choose a mood, write journal entries, receive age-friendly writing prompts, collect badges, follow writing streaks, and create a local PDF journal book.

The app is designed for the 6-8 and 9-12 age groups. It focuses on privacy and child safety. There are no ads, no subscriptions, no account creation, no cloud backup, no Firebase/API/server storage, no public sharing, no child-to-child messaging, and no location, camera, microphone, or contacts permission. Journal entries and personalization data are stored locally on the device, and local app data can be deleted from Settings with the parent password.

The Parent Area is protected with a parent password. The default parent password is 1234 and can be changed in the app. If the password is forgotten, there is no account/server recovery; the app can reset the password to 1234 by deleting all local data. Premium is a one-time in-app purchase (`premium_lifetime`) managed through Google Play Billing and is only managed from the Parent Area. The app does not store payment card or payment information, and child-facing screens do not show aggressive purchase calls. Local Premium status can be cleared when all local data is deleted, and a real purchase can be restored through Google Play.
