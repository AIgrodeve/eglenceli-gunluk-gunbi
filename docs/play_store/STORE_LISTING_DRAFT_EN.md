# Store Listing Draft EN

## App title

Fun Diary

## Short description

A safe and playful journaling app that helps children build a daily writing habit with Gunbi.

## Note

This English text is a draft for future multilingual store listing preparation. The app name may remain Turkish as "Eğlenceli Günlük".

## Long description draft

Fun Diary is a warm and simple journaling app designed to help children build a writing habit and express their feelings safely. With Gunbi, children can choose a mood, write journal entries, receive age-friendly writing prompts, collect badges, follow writing streaks, and create a local PDF journal book.

The app focuses on privacy and child safety. There are no ads, no public sharing, no child-to-child messaging, and no location permission. Journal entries are stored locally on the device, and local app data can be deleted from Settings.
